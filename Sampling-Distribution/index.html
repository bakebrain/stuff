Sampling Distribution of the Sample Mean

bakebrain

What?

Imagine that we would draw a large amount of samples from a population. For each sample we will compute the mean. The distribution of that statistic is called the sampling distribution.

The population

The application lets you define an initial population. First, choose a distribution:

  • Normal
  • Unifrom
  • Log-normal
  • Exponential

Options

  • Number of observatinos - number of observation of the population
  • Sample size - number of observations in the sample
  • # of samples: how many samples are drawn ("replications")

Central Limit Theorem

"The central limit theorem states that the sampling distribution of any statistic will be normal or nearly normal, if the sample size is large enough."

With the given application you can verify this theorem. Choose a non normal distribution as the original population form which the samples are drawn. Play with the sample size and the other factors and you will see that the resulting distribution appears to be normally distributed.
